Privacy Policy for Split Settle

Effective Date: July 19, 2025
Last Updated: July 19, 2025

1. Introduction and Overview

Welcome to Split Settle ("we," "our," "us," or the "App"). Split Settle is a financial tracking application designed to help users manage shared expenses, track debts, and settle payments among friends, family, roommates, and groups. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your financial and personal information. This policy applies to all users of Split Settle, regardless of how you access or use our services.

Important: By using Split Settle, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our privacy practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us (User-Generated Content)

Split Settle operates on a user-input model, meaning most of the data in our system is voluntarily provided by you. We collect the following types of information that you choose to input:

Transaction Information:

  • Expense Details: Transaction amounts, descriptions, categories, dates, and payment methods
  • Bill Splitting Data: Information about how expenses are divided among participants
  • Payment Records: Details of settlements and payments between users
  • Receipt Information: Photos or scanned images of receipts (if you choose to upload them)
  • Currency Preferences: Your preferred currency and exchange rate information

Contact and Social Information:

  • Friend/Group Data: Names, email addresses, and phone numbers of people you add to your groups or transactions
  • Group Information: Group names, descriptions, member lists, and group settings
  • Communication Data: Messages, comments, or notes you add to transactions or send through the app

Account Information:

  • Profile Data: Your name, email address, phone number, profile picture, and display preferences
  • Authentication Information: Username, password (encrypted), and security question responses
  • Verification Data: Information used to verify your identity when creating an account

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use Split Settle, we may automatically collect certain technical and usage information:

Device and Technical Information:

  • Device Identifiers: Unique device ID, advertising ID, and hardware information
  • Operating System: OS version, device model, and manufacturer
  • App Usage Data: Features used, time spent in app, crash reports, and performance data
  • Network Information: IP address, ISP, and connection type

Location Information:

  • Approximate Location: City, state, and country based on IP address
  • Precise Location: GPS coordinates (only if you grant permission and enable location-based features)

2.3 Information from Third-Party Sources

  • Social Media Integration: If you connect social media accounts, we may receive basic profile information
  • Contact Synchronization: If you grant permission, we may access your device's contact list to help you find friends using Split Settle
  • Payment Processor Data: Transaction confirmations and payment status from integrated payment services

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary App Functions

We use your information primarily to provide and improve Split Settle's core functionality:

  • Expense Tracking: Calculate, display, and manage shared expenses and individual balances
  • Debt Management: Track who owes what to whom and provide settlement recommendations
  • Group Management: Create and maintain groups for shared expense tracking
  • Payment Processing: Facilitate payments and settlements between users (when integrated with payment services)
  • Data Synchronization: Sync your data across multiple devices and with other users in your groups
  • Backup and Recovery: Maintain secure backups of your data for recovery purposes

3.2 Communication and Notifications

  • Transaction Notifications: Alert you and other users about new expenses, payments, and balances
  • Reminder Messages: Send payment reminders and settlement notifications
  • App Updates: Inform you about new features, updates, and important changes
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance

3.3 Personalization and Analytics

  • User Experience: Customize the app interface and features based on your preferences
  • Usage Analytics: Analyze app usage patterns to improve performance and user experience
  • Feature Development: Use aggregated data to develop new features and improve existing ones
  • Fraud Prevention: Monitor for suspicious activity and protect against unauthorized access

3.4 Legal and Compliance

  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Dispute Resolution: Resolve disputes between users regarding transactions or payments
  • Security Monitoring: Detect and prevent security breaches and fraudulent activities

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Sharing Within the App

Split Settle is designed for shared financial tracking, which means certain information is shared by design:

  • Group Members: Transaction details, balances, and payment information are visible to other members of the same group or expense
  • Transaction Participants: Users involved in specific transactions can see all details related to those transactions
  • Settlement Information: Payment confirmations and balance updates are shared with relevant users
Note: You have control over what information you input and which groups you join. We only share information that is necessary for the app's expense-sharing functionality.

4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating Split Settle:

Service Type Purpose Data Shared

Analytics Services App performance and usage analysis Aggregated, anonymized usage data

4.3 Legal Requirements and Safety

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal processes, court orders, or government requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service or other agreements
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of Split Settle, our users, or the public
  • Investigate fraud, security breaches, or other illegal activities
  • Respond to emergency situations involving danger to life or property

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction. We will notify you via email or app notification before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Security and Protection

5.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication options and secure password requirements
  • Access Controls: Role-based access controls and principle of least privilege
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and software updates applied promptly
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and intrusion detection systems

Organizational Safeguards:

  • Employee Training: Regular security training for all team members
  • Background Checks: Security screening for employees with data access
  • Incident Response: Comprehensive security incident response procedures
  • Vendor Management: Security assessments of all third-party providers

5.2 Data Backup and Recovery

  • Regular automated backups with geographic redundancy
  • Point-in-time recovery capabilities
  • Disaster recovery procedures and business continuity planning
  • Regular backup integrity testing and restoration procedures

5.3 Security Limitations

While we implement robust security measures, no system is completely secure. You can help protect your information by:

  • Using a strong, unique password for your Split Settle account
  • Enabling two-factor authentication when available
  • Keeping your app updated to the latest version
  • Not sharing your login credentials with others
  • Logging out of shared devices
  • Reporting suspicious activity immediately

6. Data Retention and Deletion

6.1 Retention Periods

We retain your information for different periods based on the type of data and business necessity:

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Transaction Records 7 years after account closure Legal compliance, dispute resolution
Account Information Duration of account + 2 years Account management, support
Communication Data 3 years from last activity Support, feature improvement
Usage Analytics 2 years (aggregated/anonymized) App improvement, performance analysis
Device/Technical Data 1 year from last app use Technical support, security

6.2 Account Deletion

When you delete your Split Settle account:

  • Immediate Actions: Your profile becomes inaccessible, and you're removed from all groups
  • 30-Day Grace Period: Account can be restored within 30 days of deletion request
  • Complete Deletion: After 30 days, most personal data is permanently deleted
  • Retained Information: Some transaction records may be retained for legal compliance
  • Shared Data: Information in shared transactions remains visible to other participants but your personal identifiers are anonymized

6.3 Data Portability

Before deleting your account, you can request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format. This includes:

  • All your transaction history and records
  • Group memberships and roles
  • Account settings and preferences
  • Communication history within the app
  • Balance and payment records
